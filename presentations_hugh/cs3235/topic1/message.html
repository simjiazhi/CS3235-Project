It is the year 3008: a period of complexity and confusion. The residents of the rebel planet Earth-2 are concerned for their future.

To buoy their spirits, Kaumatua Kiwi has asked them to participate in a module: DAAAD: The dark arts, attacks and defences!.

This two-week course aims to expose students to issues in computer security. The course will provide students with a broad introduction to the underlying issues and techniques which result in our computer systems being less reliable, and exposed to risk.

It will cover attack surfaces for a range of computer equipment, not just the ubiquitous PC.

For example, we will also look at attacks on GSM phones, and the infrastructure used to support our public utilities (the wonderful world of SCADA and PLCs). In addition to the focus on attack surfaces, the course will examine the defences against each attack, providing practical guidance as to how to make you, your organization, and your country safer.
